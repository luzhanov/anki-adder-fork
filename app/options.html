This extension supports shortcuts for quickly adding marked text to each field, without the need to first open the popup window.

Example of it's usage:

  1. Look up a word in you favourite online dictionary.
  2. Mark the word and press e.g. Alt+1 to add it to the front field.
  3. Mark the translation/definition and press e.g. Alt+2 to add it to the back field.
  4. Mark any extra information, such as example sentences, and add each further down in the same field with (again) Alt+2, or in a third field with e.g. Alt+3.
  5. Open the popup with e.g. Alt+A and click the "Add Card" button.

How to customize shortcuts:

Go to the extensions page and press "Keyboard shortcuts" at the bottom of the page.
If there are certain decks or card models you know you will never use with this extension, you can hide them from the drop-down menus to make it easier to select the decks and models you do use.
